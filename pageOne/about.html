Our Journey

What started as a small garage project fueled by a love for drifting has grown into a full-scale bodyworks company trusted by drivers worldwide. In the early days, we spent countless nights designing and testing custom kits, learning what truly makes a car ready to slide with confidence. Every dent, weld, and tweak became part of our story. Over the years, our dedication to precision engineering and bold design transformed us from local enthusiasts into innovators in the drift community. Today, we continue to push boundaries—combining craftsmanship, motorsport knowledge, and creativity to deliver bodyworks that don’t just perform, but inspire. From grassroots events to international competitions, our journey is a reflection of passion, persistence, and the drive to keep evolving—just like the sport of drifting itself.
